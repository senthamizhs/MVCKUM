Subscriber Agreement – Point of Sale & Financial Settlement

This Subscriber Agreement is being made on this day of  #Current_Date# between TravRays Travel Technology LLC (hereinafter referred as “TravRays”) and #Agency_Name# (hereinafter referred as “Subscriber”) in Dubai, United Arab Emirates for the purposes of using TravRays Point of Sale (POS) application for travel transactions. The terms and conditions of the agreement is drafted below:

  1. TravRays Point of Sale (POS)

TravRays will provide the subscriber with a POS application for the use of travel transactions which may include flights, hotels, car, other ground transportation services and any other services, procured from its Travel Supplier Partners, who are registered within the travel industry, for the purpose of fulfilment of the said transaction. POS will be a single application delivery to the subscriber with required support and service. TravRays may have updates to the POS on a regular basis. Based on subscriber’s performance and the need, customized version delivery can be made available.

  1. Reports

TravRays is committed in delivering the subscriber with all the reports on every transaction through an inbuilt tool within the POS. Application has been built to deliver reports time to time basis with date ranges. Subscriber will also have the ability to get these reports from TravRays office in Dubai during the working hours of 09:00 AM to 05:00 PM from Sunday through Thursday.

  1. Support and Service

TravRays will have a dedicated support center in Dubai, UAE. Subscriber can reach the support team on 04 – 236 4441. Subscribers will also be able to reach the account manager for support queries.

  1. Financials

    1. Accounting management

POS has been designed to have a Top Up model, wherein the subscriber can add funds to his account via a bank transfer, cash deposit or credit card. On receipt of funds in TravRays account the value will be reflected in the POS of the subscriber. Subscriber will have the ability to know the balance of funds on an instantaneous basis and can verify the transaction through the online reports.

    1. Commission settlement

Commissions agreed between TravRays and subscriber will uploaded into the POS, wherein the subscriber will be able to view at the time of sale. Report from the POS will have the commission information and subscriber will have access to this information anytime.

    1. Refunds & Adjustments

In case of any sales refunds and adjustments related to cancellation or reschedule, subscriber can use the POS for such transaction. Subscriber can also reach out to the Help Desk or the account manager for the settlements. TravRays is committed in managing and displaying all customer travel and financial transactions in the POS.

  1. Transaction Security

TravRays will have its best efforts on all the travel and financial transactions coming from the subscriber. All the required information will be made available to the subscriber, in case of any failure, which is beyond the control of TravRays.

  1. Disputes & Differences

Any disputes or differences arising due to transactions, it will be settled in an amicable way. Failure in reaching an agreement, either party will have the rights raise this with the court of law in UAE.

  1. Validity & termination of the agreement

This agreement is only valid for a period of 3 years from the date of signature. Based on the mutual understanding and relationship, the agreement extension will be discussed between TravRays and subscriber.

  1. LIMITATION OF LIABILITY

Under any and all circumstances, TravRays liability is limited to the extent of the fee charged by TravRays for this assignment. TravRays does not bear responsibility to compensate any person or update this report in respect of any event arising out of TravRays POS covered in this report.

  1. Force Majeure

Neither TravRays nor #Agency_Name# shall be liable in any way for failure in performing their respective obligations under this agreement if the failure is due to causes outside the reasonable control of the party in default.

  1. Electronic Signature

This agreement has been agreed between TravRays Travel Technology LLC and the Subscriber for an electronic signature as acceptance to the agreement.
